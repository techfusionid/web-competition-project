1. Definisi Value Proposition

  - Problem: Orang susah mencari info lomba yang relevan
  - Solution: Satu platform yang mengumpulkan semua kompetisi dalam satu tempat
  - USP: Terlengkap, terupdate, kategori lengkap, mudah dicari

  2. Segmentasi Target Audiens

  Primary Users:
  - Pelajar/Mahasiswa yang cari beasiswa & pengalaman
  - Job seeker yang ingin portofolio
  - Professional yang cari challenge & networking

  Secondary Users:
  - Penyelenggara lomba (corporate, komunitas, universitas)
  - HR/Recruiter yang mencari talent

  3. Channel Marketing

  Core Channel - WA Channel:
  - Build community di WhatsApp Channel (utama)
  - Daily update lomba terbaru via automasi dan human review
  - Exclusive tips & resources
  - Direct engagement dengan community

  Launch Tactic - Thin Promotion:
  - LinkedIn: Professional build in public, journey sharing, networking dengan komunitas tech/education
  - Pendekatan: Soft launch, organic growth, no hard selling

  Organic Channels (Secondary):
  - SEO: Keyword "lomba 2025", "kompetisi nasional", "hackathon Indonesia", "info lomba gratis"
  - Content Marketing: Blog tips menang lomba, story pemenang, guide ikut lomba
  - Community: WA Channel sebagai home base utama

  Partnership:
  - Kampus BEM/UKM (untau distribusi ke WA Channel)
  - Komunitas tech/design
  - Peer-to-peer sharing (word of mouth)
  - Media partner (portal berita kampus)

  4. Content Strategy

  WA Channel Content:
  - Senin: Weekly roundup lomba minggu ini
  - Rabu: Deep review lomba menarik (deadline dekat, prize menarik)
  - Jumat: Tips & tricks menang lomba
  - Weekend: Success story pemenang
  - Daily: Quick update lomba baru

  Threads Content (Launch Phase):
  - Build in public: Progress development, challenges
  - Behind the scenes: How the platform works
  - Casual updates: New competitions added
  - Tips singkat: Quick value posts
  - No hard promotion, focus on value & authenticity

  LinkedIn Content (Launch Phase):
  - Professional journey: Why I built this
  - Problem-solution: Challenges finding competition info
  - Networking: Connect with tech/education community
  - Thought leadership: Competition/career development
  - Soft promotion: Share value, not selling

  Format:
  - WA: Text + link, occasional image
  - Threads: Short text, visual, thread series
  - LinkedIn: Professional, value-driven posts

  5. Growth Tactics

  Pre-Launch:
  - Build WA Channel community dari network personal
  - Onboard 20-30 kompetisi awal (reach out penyelenggara)
  - Teaser di Threads & LinkedIn (build in public)
  - Collect feedback dari early users
  - Soft opening: Invite only, word of mouth

  Launch (Soft Launch):
  - Announcement di WA Channel dulu (main community)
  - Threads posts: "Finally launched" journey post, soft promo
  - LinkedIn post: Professional announcement, value-focused
  - No big campaign, no PR, no giveaway
  - Focus on quality traffic over quantity

  Post-Launch:
  - Consistent WA Channel content (daily/weekly)
  - Weekly Threads update (what's new, behind the scenes)
  - Monthly LinkedIn reflection post (learnings, metrics)
  - Referral: Natural word of mouth dari satisfied users
  - Community driven: User share ke teman2 mereka
  - Iterate berdasarkan feedback

  Focus:
  - Community growth di WA Channel
  - Authentic engagement di Threads/LinkedIn
  - Quality over quantity
  - Sustainable, organic growth

  6. Monetization Strategy (kalau needed)

  - Premium listing untuk penyelenggara
  - Featured competition
  - Email blast ke database user
  - Workshop/online class persiapan lomba

  7. Key Metrics to Track

  Community Health:
  - WA Channel followers growth
  - WA Channel engagement rate (reaction, reply)
  - Active users vs passive users

  Platform Metrics:
  - Competition listing clicks
  - User acquisition (organic traffic)
  - User retention (returning users)
  - Session duration & pages per session

  Social Media (Secondary):
  - Threads engagement (likes, replies, reposts)
  - LinkedIn engagement (reactions, comments)
  - Profile visits & followers growth

  Success Indicators:
  - Word of mouth referrals (track via survey/feedback)
  - Natural inbound links (backlinks)
  - Repeat usage rate
  - Community satisfaction (feedback score)
